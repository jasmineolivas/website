The Dead Fall in Line

The frost seeps in sometime after midnight, ushering in the final omen that can no longer be ignored.

Carisis has a picturesque life he never thought he’d deserve: a wife, a small child, and another on the way. But he is about to be faced with an enemy from his past: The Witch. He knows all about her dark plot. The deceit. The death. The years-long war campaign that she survived at the cost of thousands of lives.

Old friends will be plunged into a dire crusade where they must contend with the harsh reality of the war machine and a growing cult consumed by blind loyalty.

To survive, they must uncover who truly holds power - and resist the darkness that waits with open arms.
