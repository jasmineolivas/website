About the Author

Jasmine Olivas is a fantasy author and illustrator weaving dark, magical worlds filled with grit, grace, and haunting beauty. When she’s not writing or sketching, she’s probably fussing over typography or getting lost in old folklore. Her stories explore resilience, transformation, and the raw edges of humanity.
